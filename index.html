Information and communication technology (ICT) can help launch and manage a business efficiently. Even though certain let may not be Immediately available in some communities, it is important to have at Least a basic knowledge of their potential to improve efficiency and expand a business.This topic introduces (ICT) in a progressive fashion, starting, with the established technologies and leading to contemporary ones.
